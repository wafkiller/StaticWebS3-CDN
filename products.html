Fiberglass casting

Woven fiberglass mesh impregnated with polyurethane, Mainly used as a substitute of plaster cast used for supporing broken bones. Activated by submersion in water. Easy to use, lightweight, yet durable.

Key features

  • Excellent lamination
  • Lightweight and easy mobility
  • Stretches to confirm best fitting cast
  • Porous weave allows skin to breath
  • Low tack resin allows easy unwinding during application
